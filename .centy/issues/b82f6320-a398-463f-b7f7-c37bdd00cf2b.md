# Split convert_entity.rs into per-domain conversion modules

## Problem

`src/server/convert_entity.rs` (117 lines) converts 4 unrelated entity types in one file:
- `GenericItem` ŌåÆ Proto
- `User` ŌåÆ Proto
- `User` ŌåÆ `GenericItem` Proto
- `Config` ŌåÆ Proto

Each conversion is independent and unrelated to the others.

## Proposal

Move each conversion closer to its domain:
- `server/convert/item.rs`
- `server/convert/user.rs`
- `server/convert/config.rs`

Or alternatively, co-locate them with their domain modules (e.g. `item/proto_convert.rs`, `user/proto_convert.rs`).
